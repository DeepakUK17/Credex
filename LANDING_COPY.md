## FAQ — 5 Q&As

**Q: Is this actually free? What's the catch?**  
A: Yes, completely free. No account, no credit card, no catch. We're Credex — we negotiate AI vendor credits for startups. The audit tool helps us find the teams who need us most. If your savings are over $500/month, we'll suggest a consultation. If not, you still get the full report.

**Q: How accurate is the pricing data?**  
A: We verify every price directly from the official vendor pricing page before each major release. The audit tool timestamps its data so you know exactly how fresh it is. All sources are documented in our public PRICING_DATA.md file in the GitHub repo.

**Q: Will my data be shared or sold?**  
A: No. Your spending data is stored in Supabase with a random UUID — no name, no email attached unless you choose to submit the lead form. We never sell data. The shareable audit URL only shows tool names and savings; it strips all personal information.

**Q: What if I use tools not on the list?**  
A: The current v1 audit covers the 8 most common tools used by engineering teams. Add your tool to our waitlist via the lead capture — we'll notify you when we add it. Perplexity, Notion AI, and GitHub Models are next on the roadmap.

**Q: How is this different from just reading the pricing pages myself?**  
A: Three things you can't easily do yourself: (1) We apply cross-tool logic — e.g., comparing your Cursor Pro spend to Copilot Business for your specific seat count. (2) We surface plan-fit issues — most teams don't realize there's a cheaper plan for their team size. (3) We estimate Credex credit opportunities, which aren't publicly advertised.
